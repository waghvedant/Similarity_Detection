WHAT IS PLAGIARISM?

Plagiarism is the practice of taking someone else's work or ideas and passing them off as one's own .

Due to the rapid growth of techonologies , there is been rush among the people about carrying out research on various innovative ideas and then publishishing it by achieving credibility for it; But when it comes to the originality of their published work there has to something which will verify that the content is not copied and duplicated.
